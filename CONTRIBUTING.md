# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in contributing!

Before any contribution can be accepted, you must sign the Contributor License Agreement (CLA).

By signing the CLA you confirm that:
- You are the author of your contribution, and
- You grant the project owner the right to use, modify, relicense, and distribute your contribution.

Contributions without a signed CLA cannot be merged.

See the CLA in the file `CLA.md`.
